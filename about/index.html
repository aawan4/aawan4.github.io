About Us

WeAR Galaxy is an ambitious project spearheaded by a dedicated team of software engineering students from the University of Central Punjab (UCP). With a shared passion for technology and innovation, the team is working tirelessly to redefine how people experience eyewear shopping. By integrating cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) technology, WeAR Galaxy aims to eliminate the guesswork and inconvenience of traditional shopping methods. The app provides users with an immersive virtual try-on experience, allowing them to see exactly how different glasses look and feel on their faces, all from the comfort of their homes. This initiative not only bridges the gap between fashion and technology but also aspires to set new standards in the eyewear industry. With a strong focus on usability, creativity, and seamless functionality, the team is determined to make eyewear shopping more engaging, efficient, and enjoyable for users worldwide. WeAR Galaxy embodies a vision of blending style with innovation, creating a future where trying on glasses is as simple as a tap on your screen.
